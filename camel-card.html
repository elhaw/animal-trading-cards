The Camel

Camels have been domesticated for3,000 years, according to the San Diego Zoo, and used for transportation, meat, fur, leather and milk.

  • average life expectancy: 40 to 50 years
  • Speed: 25 mph to 67mph
  • Average weight: 1,320 to 2,200 lbs
  • Habitat: desert, prairie or steppe

Camels are mammals with long legs, a big-lipped snout and a humped back. There are two types of camels: dromedary camels, which have one hump, and Bactrian camels, which have two humps.
